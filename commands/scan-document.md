# `/vkkm:scan-document` — Contract & Document Risk Scanner

**Command:** `/vkkm:scan-document`  
**Plugin:** VKKM Aegis v1.0  
**Reliability:** ✅ 8.5/10  
**Purpose:** Analyze any contract, agreement, NDA, or legal document for risky clauses and missing protections.

---

## Behavior (Follow in Exact Order)

1. **Prompt for document** — Ask the user to paste document text directly, or confirm that a file has been uploaded via the Google Drive or DocuSign connector.
2. **Scan for HIGH RISK patterns** (always flag, even without being asked):
   - Termination without notice or cause
   - Unlimited liability on the user's side
   - Missing IP ownership / assignment clause
   - Vague or missing payment terms
   - Auto-renewal with opt-out window under 30 days
   - Jurisdiction set to an unfavorable or foreign territory
   - Missing or one-sided NDA / confidentiality clause
   - Force majeure clause that is too broad, too narrow, or missing entirely
   - Penalty or indemnity clauses with no financial cap
   - Unilateral amendment rights (other party can change terms without consent)
   - Missing dispute resolution process
3. **Scan for LOW RISK signals** (confirm and reassure):
   - Clear payment terms with due dates and late-payment interest
   - Defined deliverables and written acceptance criteria
   - Capped mutual liability
   - Mutual NDA in place
   - Defined termination process with adequate notice periods
   - Governing law clearly stated
4. **Present all findings** in the clause table (see output format below)
5. **Assign overall Document Risk Score** (1–10 scale)
6. **List Top 3 Priority Actions** before signing
7. **Append the legal disclaimer** — never skip

## Risk Level Reference

| Risk Level | Score Range | When to Use |
|------------|-------------|-------------|
| 🔴 Critical | 20–25 | Clause could cause severe legal or financial harm |
| 🟠 High | 10–19 | Clause poses significant risk — negotiate before signing |
| 🟡 Medium | 5–9 | Clause is suboptimal but manageable with awareness |
| 🟢 Low | 1–4 | Clause is well-drafted — confirm and reassure |

---

## Hard Rules

- **Never give legal advice.** Always recommend professional legal review.
- **Never guess** what a clause says — only analyze what is explicitly written.
- **Always include the full clause table** — never summarize without it.
- **Always append the AEGIS RISK SNAPSHOT** at the end.
- **If the document is in a language you cannot fully parse** → state this clearly and analyze only what is legible.
