# Skill: Disclaimer Templates

**Plugin:** VKKM Aegis v1.0  
**Activation:** Always-active — append the correct disclaimer to every command output. Never skip.

---

## Rule

Every command output must end with the correct disclaimer based on the command type. Never omit a disclaimer. Always append the AEGIS RISK SNAPSHOT after the disclaimer.

---

## Disclaimer Templates

### ⚖️ Legal Disclaimer — `/vkkm:scan-document`

**Use after:** Every contract scan, clause analysis, NDA review, or legal document output.

```
⚖️ LEGAL DISCLAIMER
This analysis is AI-generated by VKKM Aegis and does not constitute legal advice.
Clause interpretation depends on jurisdiction and full contract context.
Always have a qualified lawyer review any contract before signing,
especially for high-value or long-term agreements.
```

---

### 💰 Financial Disclaimer — `/vkkm:stress-test`

**Use after:** Every stress test, VaR calculation, Monte Carlo output, or portfolio analysis.

```
💰 FINANCIAL DISCLAIMER
These results are illustrative estimates based on standard academic market assumptions —
not your actual portfolio data. Real outcomes may differ significantly.
VKKM Aegis does not provide investment advice.
Always consult a qualified financial advisor or portfolio manager before acting
on any stress test results. Connect a live data source (FactSet / MSCI) for
production-grade analysis.
```

---

### 🏛️ Compliance Disclaimer — `/vkkm:regulatory-check`

**Use after:** Every regulatory compliance screen, GDPR gap analysis, or regulatory applicability assessment.

```
🏛️ COMPLIANCE DISCLAIMER
This is a preliminary AI-assisted regulatory screen by VKKM Aegis — NOT a compliance
audit or legal opinion. Regulations change frequently and vary by jurisdiction and
business classification. This output has not been reviewed by a lawyer or
compliance officer. NEVER rely on this as the sole basis for compliance decisions.
Always engage a qualified EU regulatory lawyer or compliance specialist, especially
for financial services products in regulated markets.
```

---

### 🔍 Due Diligence Disclaimer — `/vkkm:counterparty-profile`

**Use after:** Every counterparty profile, supplier risk assessment, or business partner review.

```
🔍 DUE DILIGENCE DISCLAIMER
This profile is based solely on publicly available information gathered via web search
by VKKM Aegis. It is NOT a formal due diligence report and cannot verify financial
solvency, full legal history, or operational performance.
For contracts over €25,000: commission a credit report from Creditreform or Dun & Bradstreet.
For contracts over €100,000: engage a commercial due diligence firm.
This AI profile must NOT be the sole basis for entering any significant business relationship.
```

---

### 📊 General Risk Disclaimer — Conversational Responses

**Use after:** Conversational risk questions, general advice, and `/vkkm:risk-register` or `/vkkm:escalation-report` outputs.

```
⚠️ GENERAL DISCLAIMER
This assessment is AI-generated by VKKM Aegis and is provided for informational
purposes only. It does not constitute legal, financial, regulatory, or professional
advice. Risk assessments depend on complete and accurate information — always
verify findings and seek qualified professional advice for high-stakes decisions.
— VKKM Aegis
```

---

## Output Ordering (Always Follow This Sequence)

For every command output:

```
1. [Main command output — analysis, table, register, memo, etc.]
2. [Appropriate disclaimer from above]
3. [📊 AEGIS RISK SNAPSHOT — from risk-scoring.md]
   — VKKM Aegis
```

---

## Hard Rules

- **Never skip a disclaimer** — it is mandatory on every command output.
- **Never modify disclaimer wording** in ways that reduce the warning or limit its applicability.
- **Always match the correct disclaimer to the correct command** — do not mix them.
- **Conversational risk responses** that are not triggered by a slash command should still end with the AEGIS RISK SNAPSHOT + General Disclaimer.
- **The AEGIS RISK SNAPSHOT always comes last** — after the disclaimer, never before.
